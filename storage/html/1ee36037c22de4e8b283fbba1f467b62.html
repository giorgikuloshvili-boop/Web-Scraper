Glo Green Lotto is a lottery service in Nigeria, which allows Glo customers to buy their favorite lottery  tickets on the Green lotto using the Glo line and pay through airtime. Details of Games are mentioned  below 
5/90 Game and *73342# USSD Description
Winning Winning means the first 5 numbers that come out in the Green Lotto game draw. Customers can stake their bets on this set of numbers with the belief that their selected numbers will come in these 5 winning numbers.
Machine Machine number means the second set of 5 numbers to come out in the Green lotto game draw. Customers can stake their bets on this set of numbers with the belief that their selected numbers will come in this second set of 5 numbers.
Double Chance - This is a game selection where a customer can place his bet on the 2 different sets of 5 numbers (Winning and Machine) at the same time on a particular game.
Draw Time - Green Lotto has 9 draws from Mondays- Saturdays and 4 draws on Sundays. 8 of the 9 draws happen in our office (in-house). The 8:25 pm game happens in Ghana, and they are known as Ghana games. On Sundays, Ghana games are played at 7:10 pm.

5/90 Game Details

5/90 game allows players to select at least one out of ninety balls numbered 1- 90. The draws are held at 9 am, 10 am, 12 pm, 2 pm, 4 pm, 6:30 pm, 9 pm, and 10.30pm to randomly select 5 out of the 90 balls. If the numbers on the 5 balls selected by the player match any of the numbers that come out at the end of the game draw, he is a winner. The variations of games to play each day are below.

  • Direct-1:- The first number drawn is called the 1st Winning Number. (E.g. If a Player selects the number 45, he wins if 45 is the first number drawn)
  • Direct 2:- Any two numbers out of the 5 winning numbers to be drawn. (e.g. If a player selects 37 and 17, he is a winner if 37 and 17 are among the 5 numbers drawn)
  • Direct 3:- Any three numbers out of the 5 winning numbers to be drawn. (e.g. If a player selects 21, 48, and 83, he is a winner if 21, 48, and 83 are among the 5 numbers drawn) 
  • Direct 4:- Any 4 numbers out of the 5 winning numbers to be drawn. (e.g. If a player selects 21, 48, 83, and 45, he is a winner if they are among the 5 numbers drawn.)
  • Direct 5:- All 5 numbers chosen must be winning numbers. (e.g. If a player selects any 5 numbers from 1 to 90, and all 5 numbers are drawn, then he is the winner.
  • Perm-2:- This is basically selecting more than 2 Numbers and combining them in sets of 2. If a player selects numbers 10-20-30, Perm2 will give combinations of (i) 10-20, (ii) 10-30, and (iii) 20-30. Here, the winning pattern is the same as direct-2.
  • Perm 3:-This is basically selecting more than 3 Numbers and combining them in sets of 3. If a player selects numbers 10-20-30-40, Perm3 will give combinations of (i) 10-20-30, (ii) 10-20-40, (iii) 10-30-40 (iv) 20-30-40. Here, the winning pattern is the same as direct-3.
  • Perm 4:-This is basically selecting more than 4 Numbers and combining them in sets of 4. If a player selects numbers 10-20-30-40-50, Perm4 will give combinations of (i) 10-20-30-40, (ii) 10- 20-40-50, (iii) 10-30-40-50 (iv) 20-30-40-50. Here, the winning pattern is the same as direct-4
  • Perm 5:-This is basically selecting more than 5 Numbers and combining them in sets of 5. If a player selects numbers 10-20-30-40-50-60, Perm5 will give combinations of (i) 10-20-30-40-50, (ii) 10-20-40-50-60, (iii) 10-30-40-50-60 (iv) 20-30-40-50-60. Here, the winning pattern is the same as direct-5
  • Banker - against all:- This is selecting one number and pairing it with the rest of 89 numbers. (e.g. if player selects 45 against all, Means 45 his banker.) This banker must be one of the 5 winning numbers.

Got Questions?

1. HOW TO PLAY ON USSD

Dial *73342#

  • Press 1 to view play game options and follow the prompts to play your game. Advise customers to have enough airtime to pay for their game ticket before they start playing.

2. CUSTOMER UNABLE TO ACCESS *73342#

Advice customers to restart or refresh their network on their phones. If the customer is using an Android phone they can turn off and on their flight mode to refresh the network.

3. CUSTOMER UNABLE TO PLAY GAMES

Verify that the customer has enough airtime. Customers can only play games with airtime available on their phones.

4. HOW TO VIEW RESULTS

Dial *73342#

  • Press 2 to view the results or
  • Visit the Green lotto website https://greenlotto.com/Home/ResultHistory if they are using an Android phone.

5. HOW TO KNOW IF YOU HAVE WINNINGS

Dial *73342# and Press 3 to view winnings

6. CUSTOMER UNABLE TO VIEW WINNING

Advise the customer to dial *73342# and press 3 to view the winnings. If the customer still claims their winning is not reflected on the USSD portal, kindly inform them to provide the Ticket ID, Game name, Date, and Time for further information to be carried out on the issue.

7. HOW TO CHECK TICKET HISTORY

Dial *73342# Press 4 to view the history of the game played. Customer can only view the last ticket played

8. CUSTOMER WANTS TO VIEW MORE TICKET GAME HISTORY

Advice customer to call Green Lotto Customer care via 018888699.

9. CUSTOMER CAN NOT VIEW TICKET HISTORY ON USSD

Customer should provide their phone number, game name.

10. HOW TO ADD BANK ACCOUNT

Dial *73342# Press 5 to add a bank account.

11. HOW TO CASH OUT WINNINGS

Verify that Customer has added their account.

  • Dial *73342# and select option 3.
  • select option 1 for Airtime.
  • Select option 2 to transfer to the bank account.

12. CUSTOMER WISHES TO PLAY WITH THEIR WINNINGS

Customer cannot play with the winning -Customer can withdraw it as airtime to play game.

13. CUSTOMER DIDNT RECEIVE THEIR WINNINGS VIA AIRTIME

Customer should provide the number they tried crediting the Airtime into and the amount they tried to credit. Customer care can check the status of Airtime credit with these details.

14. CUSTOMER COMPLAINTS OF DELAY IN RECEIVING WINNINGS VIA MONEY TRANSFER

customer to call Green Lotto Customer care via 018888699.

Job Applicant Privacy Notice

Introduction

In this Recruitment Notice, Globacom, we, our and us refer to Globacom Limited, incorporated under the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria, with its registered office at Mike Adenuga Towers, 1 Mike Adenuga Close, Off Adeola Odeku Street, Victoria Island, Lagos. We are committed to the fair, transparent, and lawful processing of your personal data in accordance with the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA), and to safeguarding your privacy in every step of our recruitment process.

Definitions

Personal Data Any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person, including but not limited to names, contact details, identification numbers, education history, and employment information.
Sensitive Personal Data Special categories of data such as health, race or ethnic origin, religious beliefs, trade union membership, biometric data, or criminal records.
Data Subject A natural person whose personal data is being processed in this case, a job applicant.
Processing Any operation performed on personal data (collection, storage, use, disclosure, deletion) whether automated or not.
Third Party Any person or entity other than the data subject, the data controller, or those authorized to process data under the controllers direct authority.

Scope

This notice applies to all individuals applying for employment at Globacom, whether through our official careers portal, third-party platforms, or recruitment agencies.

Types of Data Collected

  • Bio data: Full name, date of birth, gender, etc.
  • Contact information: phone number, email, address.
  • Academic and professional qualifications, employment history and references, CVs, certificates, identification documents, background checks, and any other information relevant to evaluating your application.

Lawful Basis for Processing

Your personal data is processed on one or more lawful bases including: performance of a contract/pre-contractual obligations, legal obligations, consent (for sensitive personal data or retention for future roles), and legitimate interests (improving recruitment, diversity, fraud prevention). You may withdraw consent at any time where processing relied on consent.

Data Collection Methods

We collect data via our online application platform, email submissions, third-party recruitment agencies, Google Forms, and in-person or virtual interviews and assessments.

Data Use Purpose

We use your personal data to process applications, assess qualifications, conduct interviews and background checks, contact you about application status, and retain information for future recruitment where you consent.

Data Sharing & Disclosure

Personal data may be shared with internal HR and recruitment teams, third-party service providers under NDPA-compliant agreements, regulatory authorities where required, and affiliated companies for group-level recruitment purposes.

Data Retention

Personal data is kept, reviewed periodically and destroyed in line with law, the purpose it was obtained for, contract terms, statutory requirements, or upon a valid deletion request where no investigation or statutory restriction applies.

Your Rights

  • Right to request and access Personal Data held about you (including a common electronic format where structured).
  • Right to information on personal data collected and stored.
  • Right to object or request restriction, rectification, or deletion (subject to legal limitations).
  • Right to data portability.
  • Right to object to automated decision making/profiling.
  • Right to opt out of marketing and unsolicited messages.
  • Right to lodge a complaint with the Nigeria Data Protection Commission (NDPC).

Data Security & Breach Response

We use industry-standard technical and organisational measures (encryption at rest, access controls, security audit logging, physical security) to protect personal data. In case of a breach, we will contain, investigate, notify affected applicants and the NDPC within 72 hours (where required), and provide guidance to affected individuals.

Third Parties & Cross-Border Transfers

We may disclose data to service providers, law enforcement, regulators, or in corporate transactions. Cross-border transfers occur only with appropriate safeguards such as NDPC-approved instruments (SCCs, BCRs), and only when adequate protections are in place.

Changes to this Notice

Globacom may update this Recruitment Notice periodically to reflect legal, regulatory, or operational changes. Significant changes will be notified via our website or email.
