###### [How can I enjoy Glo Voice Bundle?](#)

---

Simply dial \*555# to purchase the Glo Voice Bundle of your choice and enjoy calls at discounted rates to all networks in Nigeria.

###### [Can I make international calls with my Glo Voice Bundle?](#)

---

No, Glo Voice bundle minutes are for only local calls. International calls are charged from your Main account.

###### [Can I purchase multiple Glo Voice Bundle?](#)

---

Yes, you can.

###### [What will be the priority if I have multiple bundles?](#)

---

The lower validity of the bundle will have higher priority of consumption.

###### [What if I do not have sufficient Main account balance to cover the fee for the Glo voice bundle?](#)

---

Purchasing the bundle will be impossible unless you go for the pack that your account balance can cover.

###### [Can I rollover unused minutes?](#)

---

Yes, unused minutes can be rolled over when you renew/purchase another Glo Voice Bundle of same value before the current one expires or gets exhausted. Once a bundle expires or gets exhausted before you purchase another one, the unused minutes of the older pack are lost. However, if you buy Glo Voice Bundle of different value from the existing bundle then remaining minutes will not roll over.

###### [What happens if I use up my Glo Voice Bundle before it expires?](#)

---

You can either purchase another Glo voice bundle or make calls from your Main account at the prevailing rate on your tariff plan.

###### [How do I opt out of Glo Voice Bundles?](#)

---

There is no action required of you. Once your bundle is exhausted or validity period expires, your calls will automatically be from your Main account based on the profile you are on.

###### [How do I check my Glo Voice Bundle balance?](#)

---

Simply Dial \*310\*2# to check your Bundle balance.
