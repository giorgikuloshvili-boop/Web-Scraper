###### [How does the daily data allocation work?](#)

---

You receive a fixed data amount every day (e.g., 1GB/day for N5,000 plan). The daily quota is credited at midnight and unused data rolls over to the next day within the bundle’s validity.

###### [Will my unused daily data be rolled over at the end of the day?](#)

---

Yes, any unused data from a day will roll over and be added to the next day’s allocation during the bundle validity period.

###### [Can I use the Always ON data for all internet activities?](#)

---

Yes. You can use your data for streaming, social media, browsing, gaming, downloads, and more.

###### [Can I activate another data plan along with an Always ON Bundle?](#)

---

Yes. If you have multiple active plans, your usage will be deducted first from the Always ON daily allocation, then from other available plans.

###### [Can I gift a Glo Always ON Bundle to someone else?](#)

---

Currently, Always ON Bundles cannot be gifted to other numbers. They are only for use on the subscribed line.

###### [How can I check my Always ON Bundle balance?](#)

---

Dial \*323# or use the Glo Café App or visit hsi.glo.com

###### [What happens if I exhaust my daily allocation before the end of the day?](#)

---

You can either:

* Wait until midnight for the next day's credit, or
* Buy an additional data plan to continue browsing.

###### [Will I be notified when I use up my daily quota?](#)

---

Yes. You will receive SMS notifications once your daily data quota is used up.

###### [Can I re-subscribe before my current bundle expires?](#)

---

Yes. You can renew or re-subscribe at any time via \*312#, Glo Café App, or hsi.glo.com.
