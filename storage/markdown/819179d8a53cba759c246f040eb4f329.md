##### Will I be charged any kind of Fee to migrate to new GLO Prime Plan ?

Migration into GLO Prime Plan is free. However the plan has a rental of N 1500
to N 10000 thus depending on the plan you choose you have to pay the
Monthly Subscription fees at the time of migrating.

##### What will happen to subscription services on my existing plan if I migrate to GLO Prime Plan ?

When you migrate successfully to GLO Prime, subscription services such as HSI
and value added services will remain intact.

##### What will happen to CUG service and tariff plan on my existing CUG package if I migrate to GLO Prime Plan ?

  immediately.
