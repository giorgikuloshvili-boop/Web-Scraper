## Got Questions?

###### [What are the Glo Tuesday Data Fiesta Bundles?](#)

---

The Glo Tuesday Data Fiesta Bundles are special, app-exclusive data offers that give customers extra value on data, available only on Tuesdays via the Glo Café App.

###### [What bundles are available under this offer?](#)

---

* ₦100 Data Fiesta → 500MB (valid 24 Hours)
* ₦200 Data Fiesta → 1GB (valid 24 Hours)
* ₦500 Data Fiesta → 3GB (valid 24 Hours)
* ₦900 Data Fiesta → 6GB (valid 24 Hours)

###### [Who can buy these Data Fiesta bundles?](#)

---

All Glo customers (new and existing) can subscribe to these Tuesday Data Fiesta Bundles through Glo Café app.

###### [Where can I buy the Data Fiesta Bundles?](#)

---

These bundles are available via the Glo Café App on Tuesdays only. These bundles cannot be purchased via USSD, SMS, recharge cards, or other channels.

###### [On which days can I buy these bundles?](#)

---

The Tuesday Data Fiesta Bundles are available only on Tuesday through Glo Café app.

###### [When are these Data Fiesta Bundles usable and how long is the data valid for?](#)

---

These Tuesday Data Fiesta Bundles are usable for 24 hours from the time of activation.

###### [Can I buy more than one bundle in a day?](#)

---

Yes. Customers can purchase multiple Data Fiesta bundles in one day, and each will carry its own 24-hour validity.

###### [Can I gift the Data Fiesta bundles to another Glo user?](#)

---

Yes. The Data Fiesta bundles can be gifted by any Glo Customer to any Glo line.

###### [Can I share these Data Fiesta bundles to another Glo user?](#)

---

No. The Data Fiesta bundles are not shareable.

###### [What happens when I exhaust my Data Fiesta bundle?](#)

---

If the bundle is exhausted before expiry, your browsing will fall back to your other active data bundle if you have any, else your internet browsing will stop.

###### [How will I know if my bundle is activated?](#)

---

You will receive an SMS confirmation after successful activation, showing bundle size, validity, and expiry time.

###### [Will I be notified as my bundle is being used up?](#)

---

Yes. You will receive usage alerts when you consume 50%, 75%, 90% and 100% of your bundle.
