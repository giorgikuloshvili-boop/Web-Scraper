## Got Questions?

###### [1. What is the GLO BEREKETE SMART TARIFF PLAN?](#)

---

BEREKETE SMART  is a prepaid tariff plan where new customers on the Glo network get rewarded with amazing voice and data benefits every time they recharge their Glo lines with a minimum of N100 within the first 6 months on the network for as long as they remain on the tariff plan. With BEREKETE SMART, customers can satisfy their browsing and talking needs from the same recharge.

###### [2. Who is eligible for BEREKETE SMART?](#)

---

All new customers joining Glo Network will be on BEREKETE SMART by default. Once they migrate out, they cannot migrate back into Berekete.

###### [3. What benefits do I get on BEREKETE SMART?](#)

---

BEREKETE SMART comes fully loaded with a lot of benefits for voice and data:

•A welcome bonus of N1,000 for all new customers upon successful activation of their lines. Successful activation means registering your SIM, recharging with minimum of N100 and making a first call..

•A whopping 900% bonus on every recharge to call ALL NETWORKS and to browse the Internet.

•New customers also enjoy up to 100% bonus on every data plan they buy in the first 4 months on the network.

###### [4. What are the detailed benefits that I will enjoy?](#)

---

a.N1,000 welcome bonus, upon activation of a new GLO SIM. The one-off welcome bonus can be used for voice and data as follows:

i.N800 to call all networks.

ii.N200 to use for data at the rate of N1/mb i.e. 200MB data

•You will be able to access this bonus after completing your SIM registration, recharging with minimum of N100 and making your first call.

•Both voice and data bonus are valid for 7 days from when your line is credited

•Please note that the welcome bonus is a one-off benefit, that applies on the recharge with which you activated your new SIM. Subsequent recharges will not get the N1,000 bonus, but you will be entitled to a whopping 900% bonus.

b.Depending on amount recharged, customer will receive a whopping 1000% bonus as follows:

| RECHARGE AMOUNT (N) | MAIN ACCOUNT CREDIT (N) | VOICE BONUS AMOUNT (N) | DATA BONUS VALUE (N) | DATA BONUS (MB) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| N100 | N100 | N100 | N800 | 40 |
| N200 | N200 | N200 | N1,600 | 80 |
| N500 | N500 | N500 | N4,000 | 200 |
| N1,000 | N1,000 | N1,000 | N8,000 | 400 |
| N5,000 | N5,000 | N5,000 | N40,000 | 2,000 |

•The 900% bonus (100% for Voice & 800% for Data) will be given on ALL recharge denominations from N100 to N5,000

•Any recharge >N5,000 will be given the same benefit as N5,000 recharge benefit.

•Voice bonus can be used to call ALL networks.

•Both voice and data bonuses are valid for 7 days

###### [5. How is my account balance depleted when I make national voice calls?](#)

---

Your account balance will be depleted in the following order:

• One-off welcome bonus account   
• Voice recharge bonus account    
• Main account

###### [6. Can I use my bonus for SMS?](#)

---

No, all SMS will be charged from your main account at standard SMS rate of N6

###### [7. Can I use my bonus for International Calls?](#)

---

No, international calls and SMS will be charged from your main account at the applicable rates per destination

###### [8. Can I use my bonus to subscribe for any value-added service?](#)

---

No, all value-added service subscriptions will be charged from main account at the applicable service rates.

###### [9. How do I use the Data Benefit?](#)

---

You can use the data benefit as you like i.e. for browsing, chatting, social media, uploading, downloading, etc.

###### [10. What happens if I buy a data plan while still enjoying my BEREKETE SMART data bonus?](#)

---

The data plan you bought will be paused. You will continue to enjoy the BEREKETE SMART data bonus. Once your BEREKETE SMART data bonus expired or exhausted, the paused data bundle will become active automatically and your bundle validity counter will start.

###### [11. What happens if I have an existing data plan and then recharge?](#)

---

The BEREKETE bonus data will be given in separate bucket and will be consumed on priority. Your existing data bundle will be used once you exhaust or upon expiry of the BEREKETE Bonus Data.

###### [12. Will I get BEREKETE SMART benefits if I recharge with Glo e-Top Up?](#)

---

Yes, BEREKETE SMART is available on both physical and e-recharge platforms.

###### [13. How do I know if I have received the BEREKETE Bonus?](#)

---

Confirmation messages will be sent to your phone after every recharge. In the unlikely event that a confirmation message is not received, kindly dial \*310# to view your BEREKETE bonus.

###### [14. I am already a Glo Customer, can I subscribe to BEREKETE SMART?](#)

---

No, only new customers on the Glo network can be on BEREKETE SMART. Migration to BEREKETE SMART is NOT allowed.

###### [15. What happens after 6 months?](#)

---

Your calls will continue to be charged at 22k/s from Main a/c and you will cease to enjoy the recharge bonus.

###### [16. What if I’m unable to consume all my BEREKETE Bonus within the Validity Days](#)

---

Unused bonus cannot be carried over unless another recharge is made within the 7 days bonus validity period.

###### [17. Can I migrate into BEREKETE SMART?](#)

---

No. BEREKETE SMART is for only new customers.

###### [18. What if I want to migrate to another Glo Plan?](#)

---

Simply dial the code of the plan you are migrating to. First migration within a month is free; subsequent migrations within the same month attracts a charge of N100 per migration. Note that you will lose any unused bonus upon migration, so we would enjoin you to fully utilize your bonuses before any migration.

###### [19. How can I enjoy the Bonus on data plan purchases?](#)

---

As a new GLO customer, you can enjoy up to 100% bonus when you buy a data plan. Simply dial \*312# or visit hsi.glo.com to buy a data plan of your choice; you will get the data bonus automatically. This offer is available

on all data plan purchases between N50 &amp; N5,000, for the 1 st four months of joining the network.

###### [20. Can existing customer enjoy the data plan bonus?](#)

---

Yes, so long as you are less than 120 days (4 months) old on the network.

###### [21. How much am I charged on usage from my Bonus account?](#)

---

•For the first 4 months after activation, you will be charged at 77k/s

•After 4 months on the network, you will be charged at 83k/s.

###### [22. What if I migrate out of BEREKETE SMART , would I still enjoy the bonus on data plans?](#)

---

Yes, so long as you have spent cumulatively less than 120 days (4 months) on the network.
