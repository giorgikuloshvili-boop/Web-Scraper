## My-G Data Plans FAQs

###### [What are the Major highlights of Glo My-G Data Bundles?](#)

---

Glo My-G Data Bundles is an event based data bundle that allows you enjoy access to your favourite Music & Entertainment apps such as WhatsApp, Tiktok, Snap Chat, BoomPlay, Audiomack, Instagram and GloTV at unbeatable rates.

###### [What are the My-G Data Bundle details?](#)

---

These My-G Data Bundles can be used to access only the applicable Music & Entertainment apps (WhatsApp, Tiktok, Snap Chat, BoomPlay, Audiomack, Instagram and GloTV)

| Plan Price (N) | Validity (Days) | Data Volume |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 100 | 1 Day | 400MB + 1 hour |
| 300 | 3 Days | 1GB + 1 hour |
| 500 | 7 Days | 1.5GB + 1 hour |
| 1000 | 30 Days | 3.5GB + 1 hour |

###### [Who Is Eligible For My-G Data Bundles?](#)

---

All Glo Customers are eligible to enjoy the My-G Data Bundles.

###### [How to Subscribe My-G Bundles?](#)

---

To access My-G Data Bundle, simply dial \*312# and go to My-G Data Bundles. This will display all the available My-G Data Bundles.

###### [What happens if I have only the My-G Data bundle and no other special data plans?](#)

---

You will be able to access WhatsApp, Tiktok, Snap Chat, BoomPlay, Audiomack, Instagram and GloTV only. You will not be able to access anything else. For e.g. you will not be able to access Youtube

###### [What happens if I have the My-G Data bundle and Youtube bundle and no General data plans?](#)

---

You will be able to access WhatsApp, Tiktok, Snap Chat, BoomPlay, Audiomack, Instagram, GloTV and Youtube only. You will not be able to access anything else, for example, you will not be able to access any other sites apart from this.

###### [If I have an existing data Plan or data bonus and subscribe to My-G Data Bundle(s), which data will deplete first while browsing?](#)

---

If you are browsing or accessing WhatsApp, Tiktok, Snap Chat, BoomPlay, Audiomack, Instagram and GloTV apps or sites, the depletion will happen from my-G Data Bundle and if you are accessing any other sites it will get depleted from General Data plan. For example, you have a My-G plan and a general plan of N500. When you browse Whatsapp, it will deplete from My-G bundle and if you are browsing Youtube it will deplete from General Plan.

###### [Will I have to re-subscribe to the same My-G Data Bundle(s) before my subscription expires?](#)

---

Your subscription will be auto renewed on the day of expiry as long as you have enough airtime but you will receive a notification message before renewal and after renewal if you opt for Auto-Renewal while subscribing to the plan.

###### [Will I be able to roll over my unused My-G Data Bundle data allowance?](#)

---

Yes, you will be able to auto-renewal the My-G Data Bundles when you have sufficient balance and your unused data will be rolled over.

###### [I got 1hr data along with My-G bundle, what is it about?](#)

---

When you activate the My-G Bundle, you will get 1hr extra data which can ONLY be used on WhatsApp, Tiktok, Snap Chat, BoomPlay, Audiomack, Instagram, GloTV.

###### [When will the 1hr bolt-on (IG/TikTok/Youtube) data expire?](#)

---

The 1hr (capped @ 200MB)will start counting from the moment you launch any of the applications and will expire an hour after. However, if not used, the 1hr/200MB will have the same validity with the bundles activated, for example, if you activate 400 MB valid for 1 day, the 1hr will be on the line for 1 day only and the 1hr will start counting from the moment you launch any of the applications applicable for My-G Bundles.

###### [How do I stop automatic renewal of any of the My-G Data Bundles?](#)

---

You can stop auto renewal by sending cancel plan name to 127.

###### [If I only have My-G bundle and I get a youtube link on whatsapp, will it work?](#)

---

No, it will not work. You will need a youtube pack or a general plan subscription for the youtube link to work.

###### [Can I gift these plans](#)

---

Yes. The plans can be gifted

###### [Can these plans be shared?](#)

---

Yes, these plans are shareable.

###### [If I have a My-G, WTF, Youtube and Instagram bundle, how does this get shared?](#)

---

When you share your plans with somebody, all the plans will get shared

###### [Will these plans work on modems, routers and mobile routers?](#)

---

Yes, these plans will work on any compatible device.

###### [Can I stack these data plan? Subscribe multiple times?](#)

---

Yes, you can buy these plans multiple times. Plans on the same category will get merged and max validity will be taken.

###### [What happens when I have multiple category plans? How do they get stacked?](#)

---

If you have multiple category plans, all the plans will be active and will run simultaneously, for example, if you have a General plan N500, WTF plan N25 and My-G plan N100 - All these plans will run simultaneously
