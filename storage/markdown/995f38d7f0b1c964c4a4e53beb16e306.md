## Got Questions?

###### [What Is “Mobile Number Portability” Or “MNP”?](#)

---

It’s a service that enables the subscriber keep their MSISDN when moving over from one mobile NETWORK to another.

###### [What Is Porting?](#)

---

It’s when a subscriber switches to a new mobile NETWORK without having to change numbers. Now everyone can join Glo, the winning network.

###### [What Is Porting In?](#)

---

It is when a customer joins Glo from another NETWORK with his/her existing number. Now all your friends loved ones can join you on Glo.

###### [What Is Porting Out?](#)

---

This is the act of a customer leaving our Glo NETWORK to join another NETWORK with all the digits of his/her existing number.

###### [Who's Who in Number Porting? SUBSCRIBER](#)

---

SUBSCRIBER:  This refers to the customer who owns the number to be ported.

###### [Who's Who in Number Porting? BLOCK HOLDER](#)

---

BLOCK HOLDER:  The operator to whom the number block (e.g. 0805,) containing the ported number was originally allocated by the NCC.

###### [Who's Who in Number Porting? DONOR](#)

---

DONOR: This refers to the operator which the subscriber currently uses.

###### [Who's Who in Number Porting? RECIPIENT](#)

---

RECIPIENT:  This refers to the operator which the subscriber now intends to port to.

###### [Who's Who in Number Porting? OTHER OPERATOR](#)

---

OTHER OPERATOR:  An operator who is not the Donor or Recipient for a port but who must receive the routing update so as to route calls to the ported number.

###### [Who's Who in Number Porting? NPC](#)

---

NPC:  This is the Number Portability Clearing House whose duty it is to manage ported numbers and the porting process.

###### [Who's Who in Number Porting? NPG](#)

---

NPG: Number Portability Gateway; provides a single operator-based gateway for a telephone operator to manage the MNP process.

###### [Who Can Port?](#)

---

MNP is open to Post-Paid/Pre-Paid mobile customers of all mobile network operators in Nigeria. Get your friends/loved ones to join Glo today.

###### [How Can You Benefit From MNP?](#)

---

The main benefit of MNP is freedom of choice and the competition that it generates. Now all your friends can join you on Glo.

###### [How Much Will It Cost Me?](#)

---

Porting is free. No cost, no stress. Simply get your friends and loved ones to switch to Glo, the winning network, free of charge.

###### [What Does the Subscriber Have To Do to Port?](#)

---

Porting can only be done at Glo shops / authorized dealers. It’s not available by telephone, online or other electronic means.

###### [How Long Does It Take to Port To a New Provider?](#)

---

The porting process takes a maximum of 48 hours! 48 hours only, and everyone you know can join you on Glo.

###### [Can a Subscriber Port Number More Than Once?](#)

---

Yes, but there is a restriction of a 45-day period before a subscriber can port out to another network.

###### [If I port to a new network, and get a new SIM card, after 45 days, will I follow the same procedure to return to my previous provider?](#)

---

Yes. Anytime you wish to port your number, you will have to go through the same process at the Gloworld, Glozone, authorized dealers shop or agent of Glo. You will also be issued a new SIM card.

###### [What Should a Subscriber Consider Carefully Before Porting?](#)

---

Subscriber should understand that call charges & packages offered by the new provider may differ from his/her current provider.

###### [Can a Subscriber’s Number Be Ported Without His/Her Permission?](#)

---

This is unlikely due to the SIM registration process and other security features of MNP.

###### [How do I confirm that it is my number?](#)

---

To port in your number to Glo, Proof of identity – either national id card, passport or driving licence or an officially validated photographic identity document.

###### [What if I do not have an ID card?](#)

---

If you are unable to present any id card, NIN will be required for porting process to be completed.

###### [Can A Subscriber Decide The Date And Time When His/Her My Number Is Ported?](#)

---

No. The operators will need to process and validate the porting and the subscriber will be kept informed during the process.

###### [If A Subscriber Has Credit/Airtime On His/Her Line Before Porting, Will it be Forfeited?](#)

---

Yes. Any outstanding credit on the subscriber’s current service will be forfeited at the time of porting, on a 'use it or lose it' basis.

###### [What happens to the Subscriber’s Security Deposit?](#)

---

If the customer has no outstanding debt, Security Deposit should be returned. However, any outstanding debt will be settled from the customer’s Security Deposit & any balance returned.

###### [Will the Subscriber still be able to enjoy free calls with their CUG members?](#)

---

No

###### [Will the Subscriber still enjoy His/Her current tariff?](#)

---

No

###### [Will the Subscriber still enjoy His/Her F&F discount?](#)

---

No

###### [Will I still enjoy His/Her VAS?](#)

---

No

###### [After porting, will the Service Provider Name on the Subscriber’s phone change?](#)

---

Yes

###### [What Should the Subscriber do if He/She Has A Problem Or Complaint?](#)

---

Contact His/Her new service provider by visiting any Gloworld, authorized dealer or calling any of our helplines to resolve the problem.

###### [When will porting become available on the CDMA and fixed-line Networks?](#)

---

NCC has adopted a phased approach to the launch of Mobile Number Portability service in Nigeria. The process to port from CDMA will begin as soon as the GSM porting process is completed and launched.

###### [What time of the day will the porting service be available?](#)

---

The porting service will be available very close to you at anytime.

###### [Can I change my mind during the porting process?](#)

---

You can change anytime before you send the confirmation SMS. Once the confirmation SMS is sent, you can no longer cancel your port request and porting process must progress to completion.

###### [How will I be advised of the status/progress of my porting request?](#)

---

You will be informed of the progress by text messages.

###### [What happens if my porting request is rejected or fails to complete?](#)

---

You should contact the new service provider (Glo) to resolve the problem.

###### [What happens to the SMS I send or receive while porting?](#)

---

Messages which have been sent to the customer prior to porting, but not delivered, may be lost.
