# SIMPLE RECHARGE

#### Glo Simple Recharge, an easy and secure way to top up your data or airtime from your bank account.

You can also top up your family and friends.

To do this, just connect your phone number to your bank account and dial \*805#, select the bank and the amount of airtime/Data you want to recharge.

## Got Questions?

###### [What is Glo E-top up?](#)

---

Glo E-top up is electronic recharge service to top up your Glo line with data or airtime from your bank account.

###### [How can I recharge via Glo E-top up?](#)

---

You can recharge via Glo E-top up via all digital & electronic platforms such as Mobile applications, bank USSD codes, bank ATM, bank websites, Glo Retailers, Glo World shops, Glo Café & Glo USSD code \*805#

###### [How I get started?](#)

---

Customer should get his Glo line registered with Bank or should register with mobile wallets. In addition, please ensure that your Bank account or wallet is funded before you start transacting on Glo E-top up.

###### [Who can recharge via Glo E-top up?](#)

---

All existing and new Glo E-top up customers can recharge via above mentioned Glo E-top up channels. Customers an also top up family and friends. Please ensure that:

* Customer has linked bank account with Glo line.
* Customer has E-wallet on mobile applications.

###### [Can I use E-top up service for other networks?](#)

---

No. Simple recharge is a Mobile Payment Service for airtime top up exclusively designed for Glo subscribers.

###### [Can I use E-top up for both prepaid and post-paid line?](#)

---

Yes, you can use Simple Recharge on both Post-paid and Prepaid lines to top up.

###### [Do I need a PIN to make use of Glo E-top up?](#)

---

No, you do not need any PIN since your phone number is registered with your banks. However, some banks may ask you to create a PIN for customer validation.

###### [What products are available for purchase on Glo E-top up?](#)

---

Customer can purchase Airtime, Data bundles, Glo IDD Bundles, Glo Voice Bundles, GloTV packs for their Glo lines.

###### [Can I use this service with all banks?](#)

---

Yes, E-top up service is available with all Banks, Fintechs and mobile applications.

###### [If I port my Glo Line, How can I use Glo E-top up service?](#)

---

Glo E-top up recharge has nothing to do with Mobile Number Portability. The service is available for any Glo line.

###### [I am getting a response “Your phone is not linked to your account”, What should I do?](#)

---

Subscriber should contact his/her bank to ensure the phone number is linked to their bank accounts.

###### [I am getting a response “Your financial institution is not available, please try again later”, What should I do?](#)

---

The subscriber should try again later.

###### [I am getting a response “Your financial institution does not authorize this transaction”, What should I do?](#)

---

This means that the bank host has not permitted this service on your account. Maybe your account is not linked to your Glo line. The subscribers should contact his/her bank.

###### [I am getting a response “No reply from ESME”, What should I do?](#)

---

This means the Bank services/platform may not be connecting at the time. The subscriber should try again later.
