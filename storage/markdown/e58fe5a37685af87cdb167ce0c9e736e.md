## YouTube Data Plans FAQs

###### [What are Glo YouTube Special Bundles?](#)

---

Glo YouTube Special Bundles are affordable data plans made for streaming and watching videos on YouTube. They let you enjoy more hours of entertainment at
the best rates.

###### [What plans are available under the Glo YouTube Specials?](#)

---

* ₦250 YouTube Special: 5 hours of YouTube (Max 1GB, valid for 1 day)
* ₦600 YouTube Special: 14 hours of YouTube (Max 3GB, valid for 2 days)

###### [Who can buy the Glo YouTube Special Bundles?](#)

---

All Glo customers — prepaid, postpaid, and hybrid — can subscribe to the
YouTube Special Bundles.

###### [How can I buy the Glo YouTube Special Bundles?](#)

---

You can subscribe in any of the following ways:
• By dialing \*312#, selecting “Buy Data”, “Social Bundles” and then "YouTube
Bundles"  
• Via the Glo Café App  
• Through the Money Master PSB App

###### [When can I use the Glo YouTube Special Bundles?](#)

---

You can use them anytime — day or night — within the validity period:  
N250 plan for 5 hours of YouTube (max 1GB) within the validity for 1 day  
N600 plan for 14 hours of YouTube (max 3GB) within the validity for 2 days

###### [Can I use the Glo YouTube Special data for other apps or browsing?](#)

---

No. These bundles are dedicated to YouTube streaming and cannot be used for
other websites or apps.

###### [Will my unused YouTube data roll over if I don’t finish it?](#)

---

No. The data will expire after its validity period ends and will not roll over.

###### [Can I purchase more than one YouTube Special Bundle at a time?](#)

---

Yes. You can buy the bundle multiple times if you need additional YouTube
streaming hours.

###### [How can I check my remaining YouTube Special data balance?](#)

---

You can check your balance through the Glo Café App or by dialing \*323#.
