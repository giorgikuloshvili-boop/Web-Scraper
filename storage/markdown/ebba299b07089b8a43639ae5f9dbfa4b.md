## Got Questions?

###### [What are Glo Collabo Bundles?](#)

---

Glo Collabo Bundles are special combo bundles that give you both voice minutes and data at great savings. They’re designed to help you stay connected affordably — whether you’re calling or browsing.

###### [Who can subscribe to the Glo Collabo Bundles?](#)

---

All new and existing Glo Prepaid customers are eligible to subscribe to the Glo Collabo Bundles.

###### [How do I buy a Glo Collabo Bundles?](#)

---

Simply dial \*312#, select “Collabo Bundles,” and choose your preferred Collabo Combo plans. Please note: The Pack Value will be debited from your Main Account Balance

###### [What can I use the voice minutes for?](#)

---

The minutes can be used to make All-Net local calls only. All other transactions like SMS, IDD, PayGo data browsing will be charged from main account balance.

###### [Can I use the allocated data on any website or app?](#)

---

Yes. The data included in your pack can be used for browsing, streaming, chatting, or social media — on any website or app.

###### [Can I buy more than one Glo Collabo Pack at a time?](#)

---

Yes. You can purchase multiple Collabo bundles — your voice minutes and data will be added together, and the validity will be the highest of the two or more plans purchased.

###### [Will my pack carry over if I don’t finish it before expiry?](#)

---

Roll over of unused data after the validity period expires is applicable within the allowed grace period. Unused voice minutes will not roll over after the validity period expires, so make sure to use your voice minutes before validity period expires.

###### [How do I check my remaining balance?](#)

---

You can check your remaining voice minutes balance by dialing \*310\*2# dialing and data balance by dialing \*323#.

###### [Why should I buy Glo Collabo Bundles?](#)

---

Because they offer amazing savings — you get both voice and data in one bundle. It is convenience, value, and reward — all in one pack!
