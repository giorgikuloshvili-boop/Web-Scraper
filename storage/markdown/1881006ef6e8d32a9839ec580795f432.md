## Got Questions?

###### [What are the Revised Glo Data Bundles?](#)

---

The Revised Glo Data Bundles are improved versions of Glo's top-selling data plans, now offering more data volume at the same affordable prices to give customers even better value for money.

###### [Who can buy the revised Glo Data Bundles?](#)

---

All Glo customers — prepaid, postpaid, and hybrid — can subscribe to any of the revised data bundles.

###### [How can I buy a revised Glo Data Bundle?](#)

---

You can easily buy a bundle through any of these channels:  
• Dial \*312#, select "Buy Data"  
• Use the Glo Café App (Android & iOS)  
• Visit hsi.glo.com on a Glo SIM

###### [What's new about these revised data bundles?](#)

---

Each of the data bundles now comes with increased data volumes at no extra cost, providing more browsing power for the same price you already pay.

###### [Can I use these data bundles for all internet activities?](#)

---

Yes. The bundles can be used for all internet services — browsing, streaming, gaming, social media, video calls, or downloads.

###### [How long are these data bundles valid for?](#)

---

Validity varies depending on the plan chosen. Daily, weekly, and monthly options are available — ensuring flexibility for every customer's need.

###### [Can I activate more than one data plan at the same time?](#)

---

Yes. You can buy multiple data plans. Your data usage will be deducted from the plan with the earliest expiry date first.

###### [Can I gift or share my revised data bundle?](#)

---

Yes. You can gift or share data with friends and family via \*312# or the Glo Café App.

###### [How can I check my data balance?](#)

---

You can check your data balance via:  
• Dial \*323#  
• Use the Glo Café App  
• Visit hsi.glo.com

###### [Will I be notified when my data is low or exhausted?](#)

---

Yes. You will receive SMS notifications when your data is 75% and 100% consumed.

###### [Can I roll over my unused data?](#)

---

Yes. Any unused data will roll over when you renew your plan before it expires or subscribe to another data plan within the grace period.

###### [What happens when my data bundle expires?](#)

---

Once your bundle validity ends, browsing will be stopped unless you renew, buy another data bundle.

###### [Why should I choose Glo's Revised Data Bundles?](#)

---

Because Glo gives you more data, better value, and stronger network coverage — ensuring you stay connected to what matters most, without spending more.
