## Got Questions?

###### [What is the Glo Welcome-Back Offer?](#)

---

The Glo Welcome-Back Offer is a special incentive for customers who haven’t used their Glo line for 90 days or more. Here’s what you get:

* Welcome-back Offer 1 for Data Customers
  + 500MB Free Data every month for the first 2 months (valid for 7 days)
  + Extra 500MB Bonus Data: 500MB extra data when they subscribe to any data plan of N100 or more (for 6 months)
  + Offer Validity: 6 Months.
* Welcome-back Offer 2 for Voice Customers
  + Free ₦50 Airtime Credit every month for the first 2 months (valid for 7 days)
  + 100% Bonus on recharge of every recharge ₦100 and above for 6 months
  + Offer Validity: 6 Months.

###### [Who is eligible for this offer?](#)

---

All customers who have who haven’t used their Glo line for 90 days or more.

###### [When will I get the offer?](#)

---

As soon as you re-start using your Glo line after 90 days of inactivity, offer benefits will be given.

###### [What is the validity of the free 500MB data and free N50 airtime?](#)

---

The 500MB (in Offer 1) or N50 airtime (in offer 2) is valid for 7 days from activation and will be credited on the first day of activity and again on the 31st day.

###### [Can I roll over unused data after 7 days?](#)

---

No. Any unused data will expire after the 7-day validity period.

###### [Can I extend the 7-day validity by buying another plan?](#)

---

No. The validity of the free 500MB data cannot be extended or accumulated.

###### [Can I share the free 500MB data?](#)

---

No. The free 500MB data is non-sharable and non-transferable.

###### [What is the validity of the extra 500MB data on data subscription of N100 & above?](#)

---

The extra 500MB data given on data subscription of N100 & above, will have the same validity as that of the data plan subscribed.

###### [What happens to my regular bonuses?](#)

---

You’ll still receive your usual bonuses (e.g., BEREKETE bonus) alongside this offer.

###### [Can I combine this offer with the Oga SIM offer?](#)

---

Yes! You’ll still enjoy the Oga SIM benefits, including 100% bonus data and extra 500MB on subscriptions of ₦100 or more.

###### [What happens to data I already had before reactivation?](#)

---

Your existing data will run concurrently with the free 500MB, but the free data will be used first.

###### [What if I subscribe to a plan below ₦100?](#)

---

You won’t get the extra 500MB, but you’ll still receive the free 500MB for the first 2 months.

###### [Can I use the Free N50 airtime for making all calls? What will be the burn rate for the N50 airtime.](#)

---

The free N50 airtime will be given in a separate bonus account which can be used only for All-net local calls. The Burn rate for the free N50 would be at 15K/sec.

###### [What will be the burn rate for the 100% bonus received on every N100 recharge?](#)

---

The 100% bonus will have a burn rate of 83K/sec.

###### [Can new customers enjoy this offer?](#)

---

No. It’s only for existing customers who haven’t used their Glo line for 90 days or more.

###### [How long is the offer available?](#)

---

The offer is available for 6 months.

###### [How will I know if I’m eligible?](#)

---

You’ll receive an SMS notification from Glo if you haven’t used your Glo line for 90 days.

###### [How do I activate the offer?](#)

---

Simply start using your Glo line.

###### [Can I use my current data bundles with the free 500MB?](#)

---

Yes. Both can be used, but the free 500MB will take priority.

###### [What if I use up the free 500MB before 7 days?](#)

---

You can continue using your Glo line as normal, but additional free data will only be credited the following month (if still within the offer period).

###### [Can I share or transfer the free 500MB to another Glo user?](#)

---

No. The data is non-sharable and non-transferable.

###### [What happens if I don’t use all my 500MB within 7 days?](#)

---

Unused data will not roll over. It will expire after 7 days.

###### [Can I use the free 500MB for all internet activities?](#)

---

Yes! The data works for browsing, social media, streaming, and more—except sharing or transferring.

###### [What happens after the 6-month offer period ends?](#)

---

The offer will no longer be available. Ensure you start using Glo line during this window to maximize benefits.

###### [Will this offer affect other bonuses like Oga SIM or BEREKETE?](#)

---

No. The Welcome-Back offer complements your existing bonuses.

###### [Can I combine the Welcome-Back Offer with other promos?](#)

---

Yes. The offer can be used alongside other Glo promotions.

###### [Is the extra 500MB for data plans above ₦100 a one-time benefit?](#)

---

No. You’ll get the extra 500MB every time you subscribe to a data plan of ₦100 or more within the 6-month offer period.

###### [Is the extra 500MB for data plans above ₦100 a one-time benefit?](#)

---

Each line must meet the 90-day inactivity requirement. The offer is applied individually to eligible lines.
