###### [How do I change my MiFi's Password?](#)

---

1. Connect your phone or laptop to the MiFi network.
2. Open a browser and enter **192.168.1.1** in the address bar.
3. Log in using your admin credentials (check your MiFi for details).
4. Go to Wi-Fi Settings > Change Password.
5. Set a new password and click Save.

**Tip:** Use a strong password for better security.

###### [How do I connect Multiple Devices to my MiFi?](#)

---

1. Turn on your MiFi device.
2. On your phone, tablet, or laptop, search for available Wi-Fi networks.
3. Select your MiFi’s network name (SSID).
4. Enter the Wi-Fi password.
5. Enjoy your internet connection.

Supports up to 10 devices at once.

###### [How do I change my MiFi's Network name (SSID)?](#)

---

1. Open a browser and enter **192.168.1.1** in the address bar.
2. Enter your username and password
3. Click on WiFI settings
4. Change the SSID (network name) to anything you like
5. Save changes and reconnect your devices.

**Tip:** Use a strong password for better security.
