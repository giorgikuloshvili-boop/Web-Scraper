## Got Questions?

###### [What is Oga SIM?](#)

---

Oga SIM is new Glo sim and customers who have spent less than or equal to 90 days in the network.

###### [How is it different from the current SIMs?](#)

---

Any new Glo SIM bought today is Oga SIM. Oga SIM is a promo Data Offer that offers newccustomers up to 100% extra bonus data on all regular Data bundles (up to N5000). See the detailed list below.bonus on all plans.

###### [Are these new bundles available to all customers?](#)

---

Any customer who buys a new Glo SIM will benefit from the promo. Existing customers who have never used data will also benefit when they buy data for the first time. Customers whose last data bundle expired more than 90 days ago will also benefit.

###### [I have taken a new Glo line recently (less than 120 days ago) will I continue to get the Oga SIM Data Offer of 100% extra Data on bundles from N50 to N5000?](#)

---

New Glo customers (Age on Network up to 120 days) will continue to get Oga SIM Data offer (100% extra main data) on all regular Data bundles up to N5000.

###### [Can I auto renew with a lower bundle and still get the extra bonus data benefit?](#)

---

Yes.

As you auto renew to a new plan you will you get the extra bonus data for the bundle bought.

###### [Can these plans be gifted?](#)

---

Yes. All bundles are giftable. But remember that the bonus received will depend on the status of the giftee. If the giftee is a new customer (or new/returning data customer), he will get the Oga SIM offer. If the giftee is an existing or old customer, he/she will get a standard data benefit. In other words, the bonus awarded does not depend on the status of the customer who is gifting rather it depends on the status of the giftee.

• Gifter – person buying the bundle and gifting to someone else.

• Giftee – the person that receives the bundle/gift.

###### [Can these bundles be shared?](#)

---

Yes, bundles are sharable

###### [How do I get these new bundles?](#)

---

BUndles are available via \*312# for bundle menu and selection on [glo](http://hsi.glo.com) .

###### [Will these bundles work on modems, routers and mobile routers?](#)

---

Yes, these bundles will work on any compatible device.

###### [Can I stack these data bundles? Subscribe multiple times?](#)

---

Yes, these plans will work like any HSI plan and share all the HSI features.

###### [Are these bundles auto-renewable?](#)

---

Yes. A Data bundle is considered auto-renewed if you Buy another data bundle before the expiry of the existing data plan or within 7 days of expiry.

###### [Will I get Oga SIM offer if I am using a 4G SIM on a 3G device?](#)

---

Yes, the promo is targeted at all new customers irrespective of SIM type (LTE or 3G) and irrespective of the device.

###### [What is the cost of the Oga SIM? Is it priced differently?](#)

---

The Oga SIM is your regular LTE SIM and is priced the same as regular SIM.

###### [How long is the promo for?](#)

---

The promo – as currently designed – is to last for 4 months from the date of activation.
