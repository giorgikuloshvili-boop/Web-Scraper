## Got Questions?

###### [What is the GLO Talkmasta 6 for 6 TARIFF PLAN?](#)

---

Glo Talkmasta 6 for 6 is a prepaid tariff plan for Glo prepaid customers where customers get rewarded with 6 free minutes and 50MB data for every 6 minutes of charged calls.

###### [How do I migrate to Glo Talkmasta?](#)

---

* New customers: Get a new Glo line and dial \*606#.
* Existing customers: Simply dial \*606#.

###### [Can I switch to another plan after registration?](#)

---

Yes, you can migrate to any plan of your choice on the Glo network by dialing the migration code of the plan.

###### [How do I check my Talkmasta bonus balances?](#)

---

Dial \*310\*2#

###### [What benefits do I get on Talkmasta 6 for 6?](#)

---

Customers on Talkmasta 6 for 6 enjoy unmatched Six free minutes and 50MB data with 24hrs validity for every Six charged minutes at 30k/s.

###### [How much do I pay for making calls on Talkmasta 6 for 6?](#)

---

30k/s is the call rate on Talkmasta

###### [Can I use my Talkmasta for SMS?](#)

---

No, all SMS will be charged from your main account at standard SMS rate of N6

###### [What is the validity of the free 6 minutes and 50MB?](#)

---

24hours from when you are credited with the free 6 minutes and 50MB

###### [How much do I get when I recharge my Talkmasta line?](#)

---

You will receive the face value of your recharge amount.

###### [For how long can I be on Glo Talkmasta 6 for 6?](#)

---

For as long as the product is available.

###### [Will my international calls qualify me for the 6 free minutes and 50MB?](#)

---

No, only cumulative local calls made daily qualify you for the free minutes and data.

###### [Can I use my bonus for International Calls?](#)

---

No, international calls and SMS will be charged from your main account at the applicable rates per destination

###### [What happens if I buy a voice bundle while I still have Talkmasta 6 for 6 free minutes bonus?](#)

---

Your Talkmasta free minutes will first be exhausted before your Voice Bundle minutes are consumed

###### [What happens if I buy a data plan while I still have Talkmasta 6 for 6 data bonus?](#)

---

The data plan you bought will be paused. You will continue to enjoy the Talkmasta 6 for 6 data bonus. Once your Talkmasta 6 for 6 data bonus expires or is exhausted, the paused data bundle will become active automatically and your bundle validity counter will start.

###### [How do I know if I have received the Talkmasta 6 for 6 Bonus?](#)

---

Confirmation messages will be sent to your phone after every cumulative 6 charged minutes of calls. In the unlikely event that a confirmation message is not received, kindly dial \*310\*2# to view your Talkmasta 6 for 6 bonus.

###### [What if I want to migrate to another Glo Plan?](#)

---

Simply dial the code of the plan you are migrating to. First migration within a month is free; subsequent migrations within the same month attracts a charge of N100 per migration. Note that you will lose any unused bonus upon migration, so you are enjoined you to fully utilize your bonuses before any migration.

###### [Is there a limit to the free minutes and data I can receive on Glo Talkmasta 6 for 6?](#)

---

No, there is no limit

###### [How do I know when I have earned or exhausted my Talkmasta bonus?](#)

---

You’ll receive notification

###### [What calls qualify me for Talkmasta bonus?](#)

---

All charged local calls

###### [What can I use the Talkmasta 6 minutes bonus for?](#)

---

To call all local networks..

###### [What can I use the Talkmasta 50MB bonus for?](#)

---

To browse any website.

###### [Can I accumulate my minutes in a single call or accumulated calls?](#)

---

Cumulative 6 charged minutes or single call that hits 6 charged minutes threshold automatically gets you rewarded with 6 free minutes and 50MB.

###### [Will I get Talkmasta bonus if I make calls using Borrow me credit?](#)

---

Yes, calls made from your Borrow me credit account will qualify you for Talkmasta bonus once you make calls of 6 charged minutes daily.

###### [Will I receive Talkmasta bonus if I am on roaming?](#)

---

No, only local calls from your main or Borrow me credit account will qualify for Talkmasta bonus.

###### [As a Talkmasta customer, how much will I be charged for international calls, SMS and Data?](#)

---

You will be charged at the standard applicable rates for all international calls, SMS, and data usage

###### [If I am a Glo Postpaid customer, can I migrate to GLO Talkmasta?](#)

---

No, Talkmasta is for only prepaid customers

###### [How do I check which plan I am on?](#)

Dial #100#
