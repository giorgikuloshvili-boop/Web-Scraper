###### [7. Is roaming data rollover or validity extension allowed with unused volume or by subscribing with larger Data Roaming Bundle?](#)

---

No, roaming data rollover or validity extensions are not allowed.

###### [8. Does the Data Roaming Bundle include voice calls?](#)

---

No, the Data Roaming Bundle only provides data. Voice calls are not included.

###### [9. How will I know when my Data Roaming is about to finish?](#)

---

You will receive SMS alerts when you reach 70%, 90%, and 95% of your allocated Roaming data or validity limit, helping you track usage while abroad.

###### [10. What happens if my Data Roaming Bundle runs out while browsing or making a data call?](#)

---

Once your Bundle expires, you’ll receive an SMS notification. You can then renew your bundle or continue using data at Pay-As-You-Go rates as applicable.

###### [11. What happens once my plan is exhausted while making a call or browsing?](#)

---

You will receive an SMS notification that your plan has expired. You will then have to renew it or continue and be charged at the normal PAYG tariff plan.

###### [12. What should I do if I encounter a problem while roaming?](#)

---

If you experience any issues while roaming, contact Glo Customer Care for assistance:

* Email: relationship@gloworld.com / corporatecare@gloworld.com
* Call: +2348050020121 (International call rates apply)
* Live Chat: Access support via the Glo Café Mobile App.

Our Customer Care team is available 24/7, including on public holidays.

###### [13. Can I set up auto-renewal or send a Data Roaming Bundle as a gift?](#)

---

Currently, auto-renewal and gifting options are unavailable. Once your data roaming bundle volume runs out, you’ll need to manually repurchase another data roaming bundle or continue at Pay-As-You-Go rates as applicable.
