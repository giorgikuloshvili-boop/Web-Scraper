#### VoLTE is the abbreviation of "Voice over LTE" and offers the possibility to make voice calls via the data communication of the LTE/4G mobile network.

Benefits of VoLTE:

* Better voice quality for making and receiving calls
* VoLTE calls are billed as minutes and will be the same as Voice call rates.
* All you need is 4G SIM and 4G / VoLTE enabled phone.

How do I enable VoLTE on my phone?

* Go to Settings -> Cellular -> Cellular Data Options -> Voice & Data -> Select 4G and click to enable VoLTE.
* Please make sure your phone supports VoLTE.
